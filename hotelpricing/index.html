🧾 Project Overview

The hospitality industry faces fluctuating demand, cancellations, and inconsistent pricing strategies. This project applies **Deep Learning (GRU)** to forecast room demand and suggest **optimal prices** that maximize revenue and occupancy.

  • Dataset: Hotel Booking Demand (Kaggle)
  • Model: GRU Neural Network for sequential forecasting
  • Goal: Predict ADR (Average Daily Rate) & Demand to guide pricing

🧠 Modeling Approach (GRU)

We used the Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU) model — a type of Recurrent Neural Network ideal for time-series forecasting. GRU captures **temporal booking trends** with fewer parameters and faster training than LSTM, making it efficient for real-time hotel pricing systems.

  • ✅ Handles seasonal and temporal patterns in bookings.
  • ✅ Reduces overfitting vs LSTM for smaller datasets.
  • ✅ Enables dynamic, adaptive pricing in production systems.

💼 Business Value & Innovation

Beyond predictions, the GRU model enables tangible business value:

  • 💰 Dynamic Pricing Strategy: Adjust prices in real-time to match demand, increasing revenue.
  • 📈 Occupancy Forecasting: Predict low-demand periods to plan discounts or promotions.
  • 🚀 Operational Efficiency: Data-driven pricing replaces intuition-based pricing.
  • 💡 Innovation: AI-driven system integrates forecasting + optimization into a smart hotel management tool.
